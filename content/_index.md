## Designing decision systems

I'm interested in how people and organisations make decisions under uncertainty, and how those decisions can be improved.

Lately I've been thinking about:

- financial independence as a decision system
- how to allocate time once money is no longer the constraint
- building simple tools and frameworks that make better decisions easier

## Building and exploring

I'm working on:

- writing about decision systems, capital allocation, and time
- developing small tools and models to test these ideas
- understanding how expertise can scale in real-world systems

## In practice

Most of my recent work has been in:

- [AI-enabled advisory systems for smallholder farmers](https://isda-africa.com)
- designing systems that translate data into practical decisions
